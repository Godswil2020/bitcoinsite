Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process withdrawals? +
Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Cryptocurrency withdrawals may take additional time depending on network congestion, usually 1-6 confirmations.
What is the minimum investment amount? +
The minimum investment varies by package: Basic ($100), Silver ($300), Business ($500), and Premium ($1,000). Each package offers different benefits and return rates.
How do referral commissions work? +
You earn commission based on your tier: Bronze (5%), Silver (7%), and Gold (10%). Commissions are paid on every investment made by users you refer, and tier upgrades are based on the number of active referrals.
Is my investment secure? +
Yes, we use bank-level security protocols, including SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and cold storage for cryptocurrency assets. Your funds are protected by multiple security layers.
Can I cancel my investment? +
Investment packages have specific durations (30-90 days). Early withdrawal may be possible with penalties. Please contact support to discuss your specific situation and available options.
What cryptocurrencies do you accept? +
We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), Litecoin (LTC), and Bitcoin Cash (BCH). All transactions are processed securely through our integrated wallet system.
